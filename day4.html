The Discovery

⏱️ 6 min read

📖 Reading Timer (Optional)
00:00

"Please," said Professor Winters. "Let us talk. We are good people."

Dad looked at Finn. Then at the professor. "OK. Talk."

They went into the cottage. They sat by the fire.

"We look for old things," said Professor Winters. "We have been looking for something special. Something on this island."

"What?" asked Finn.

"Old books," said Tom. "Very old. From 700 years ago. Monks hid them here."

"Why?" asked Dad.

"To keep them safe," said Professor Winters. "The books are very special. They have pictures. Gold pictures. They should be in a museum. So everyone can see them."

"Why were you in our cellar?" asked Dad.

"Sorry about that," said Professor Winters. "We used the tunnel. To get to the old monastery. We didn't tell anyone. We were scared. Scared of bad people."

"Bad people?" said Finn.

"Yes," said Tom. "A man. Mr. Castellan. He takes old things. He keeps them. He doesn't share. He was looking for us."

Then they heard something. A boat!

They ran to the window.

A big boat was coming!

"Oh no," said Professor Winters. "It's him. Mr. Castellan."

"What do we do?" asked Finn.

"Please help us," said Professor Winters. "We are so close. If we find the books now, we can save them. We can put them in a museum."

Dad thought. "The tunnel. Can we get there fast?"

"Yes!" said Tom. "Ten minutes."

"We go now," said Dad. "Before he gets here."

They ran to the trapdoor. Dad moved the bookshelf. They opened the door.

"Finn," said Dad. "You stay here."

"No!" said Finn. "I want to come!"

Dad looked at him. "OK. Stay with me."

They went down. Into the tunnel. Their torches made light.

They walked fast. The tunnel was long. The walls were wet. It smelled old.

After ten minutes, they went up some stairs.

At the top was a room. An old stone room. The monastery!

"There!" said Professor Winters. She pointed.

Part of the wall was different. Newer stones.

"The books are behind those stones," she said.

Tom got his tools. He moved the stones. One by one.

Behind them, they heard something. Footsteps!

"Quick!" said Dad.

Tom worked faster. The stones came away.

Behind was a small space. In it was a wooden box.

Professor Winters reached in. She lifted the box. Her hands shook.

She opened it.

Inside were books. Old books wrapped in leather. Six books. They had gold on them. And red. And blue. Pictures of angels and animals.

"We found them," she said quietly.

"How nice," said a cold voice.

They turned around.

A tall man stood there. He wore a nice coat. Two big men were with him.

Mr. Castellan.

"Thank you," he said with a mean smile. "I will take those now."

⏱️ Time: 20 minutes

ACTIVITY 1: Make a Pretty Book Page

What is a Special Old Book?

Long ago, monks made books. They:

  • Wrote by hand
  • Drew pictures
  • Used gold
  • Made fancy letters
  • Took a long time!

What You Need:

  • Paper
  • Pencils
  • Colored pencils (yellow, red, blue)
  • Ruler

How to Make Your Page:

Step 1: Draw a border

  • Use ruler
  • Draw lines around the edge
  • Leave space in the middle

Step 2: Make a big letter

  • Choose a letter (your initial!)
  • Make it BIG
  • Make it fancy with swirls
  • Colour it
  • Use yellow for gold!

Step 3: Add patterns

  • Dots
  • Lines
  • Swirls
  • In the border

Step 4: Draw pictures

  • Small animals
  • Flowers
  • Birds
  • In the corners

Step 5: Colour it

  • Use red
  • Use blue
  • Use yellow (gold!)
  • Make it pretty!

ACTIVITY 2: Memory Game

What You Need:

  • 16 cards (cut from card)
  • Pencil

Make the Cards:

Draw these (2 of each):

  • Book
  • Monk
  • Lighthouse
  • Tunnel
  • Box
  • Gold coin
  • Professor
  • Boat

How to Play:

  • Mix cards
  • Put them face down (4 rows of 4)
  • Take turns
  • Turn 2 cards over
  • Match? Keep them!
  • No match? Turn back
  • Most pairs wins!
